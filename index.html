About Me

Brianna Rivera Alvarez is a graphic designer, video editor and creative coder. She’s a first year in college majoring in digital media art at San Jose State University. She has been passionate about art for as long as she could remember but fell in love with digital art in high school through video production and editing. She is heavily inspired by music and pop cultures. On her journey as an artist, she looks forward to meeting and working with creatively driven people.

Project One: Glitch Art Part One

When something glitches, that means that something is wrong. This is the reason why I glitched pictures of events that are taking place in Gaza and that are related to the genocide. My goal with glitching these pictures is to bring focus to areas in a different way that invokes emotion onto the audience.

Glitch Art: Part Two

Inspired by Alex Sloane, an independent artist. It presents all the titles of the songs that are on her Dear Diary album. The album itself is very feminine with a dark twist and I tried to portray that in this piece. There are artists who put out the names of their songs for their upcoming album and that’s what this is.

Project Three: Little Danny Animation

This is an animation project where I used After Effects to animate and Premier Pro for sound effects. I animated a scene of a game where the main character, Danny, goes through different floors and collects coins and a key to unlock the door. His peculiar walk and interesting floor designs are aspects of this animation that I focused on.


Project Four: Code a Custom Robot

Meet Th3o. He is a pink robot that crashlanded on Earth. This is a robot that I coded in p5j.s. With this project I focused on the perspective and the background.
